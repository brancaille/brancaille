Régis Renault

Régis Renault is the author of a rare and abundant body of work, mostly published in limited editions (100–1000 copies). Under his real name or the pseudonyms Radu Dragonescu and Caym, he has been exploring for over thirty years ancient martial arts, pancrace, occultism, criminology, and philosophy.

A discreet yet major figure in the French martial arts scene, he relaunched pancrace (pankration) in the early 1990s, contributing to the birth of mixed martial arts in France long before its official recognition.

Here is a selection of his books published between 1985 and 2017.

Martial Arts & Combat Sports

Occultism

Under the pseudonym Radu Dragonescu, Régis Renault explores the arcane realms of the occult, from magical folklore and island traditions to ancient pharmacopoeias and popular mythologies. Some of his works are the result of collaborations with Ghjuvanni Tramoni and Tatiana Jestakova, combining his esoteric vision with their deep knowledge of ancient traditions.

  • Mazerisme au Cœur de l’Ame Corse
  • Le Grimoire des Druides
  • La Pharmacie des Sorcières
  • Les végétaux et Minéraux Magiques
  • Dans les Griffes de Baba Yaga
  • Les Sorciers du Berry
  • Etude sur la Lycanthropie
  • Les Runes

Politics

Le Crépuscule de l’Empire (with Tabitha Campbell) analyses the decline of American power and the rise of new geopolitical forces.

Psychology

Le Testament de Machiavel studies power strategies and the psychological mechanisms of governance.

Philosophy

De la Phantasia et du Pneuma Stoïciens au Corps Lumineux Néo-platonicien connects Stoic tradition with Neoplatonic mysticism.

Criminology

Under the name Caym, he investigates contemporary underground networks: satanic cults and 1% biker gangs.

  • Enquête sur les Réseaux Criminels Satanistes
  • 1 % : Immersion dans les Gangs de Motards

Conclusion

Régis Renault embodies a rare intellectual approach: a passionate and rigorous author who continues to transmit forgotten or forbidden knowledge to those willing to explore the margins of history and human experience.
