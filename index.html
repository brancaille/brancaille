Welcome

Brancaille is a unique martial art combining striking and wrestling, born in the south of France. This site guides you through its history, techniques, and modern revival while respecting its secretive traditions.

About Brancaille

Originating from ancient Greek and Roman pankration, Brancaille survived Christianization by adapting its form and name while keeping its essence intact. A long tradition of clandestine practices mixing festivals and deadly duels continues to inspire modern martial arts.
